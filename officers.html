Executive Committee


Co-Presidents:
Travis Labossiere-Hickman [tjlaboss@mit.edu]
Miriam Kreher [mrathbun@mit.edu]

The Co-Presidents of the section are responsible for organizing and promoting the seminar series, producing bi-annual reports for the Visiting Committee, and representing the student body to the NSED. Either co-President is also an ex officio member of the national ANS Student Sections Committee, and are required to attend (or appoint a proxy to attend) 2 out of 3 SSC meetings per year.


Public Information Officer:
Ka-Yen Yau [mit.ans.pio@gmail.com]

The PIO is responsible for keeping records of all ANS activities and keeping the student body informed. The PIO is also custodian of the bylaws and de facto webmaster.
