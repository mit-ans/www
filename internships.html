Overview

The organizations and programs below are divided into categories depending on their primary mission. Within each description you can find program details, links to more information, and contacts in the organization as well as for any MIT students with experience there. A PDF version of this list is also available for download.

While this list is primarily for summer internship opportunities for undergraduate and master's level students, the contact information here is valuable for seeking full-time employment as well.

If you have corrections or additions for this listing, please email the ANS webmaster with the information.

  • Previous Participant Contact: N/A
  • Applicants need to be U.S. Citizens or have Permanent Residency status
  • Undergraduate/Graduate: Engineering BS or MS graduate preferred; Creare is pleased to accept junior or senior undergraduate applications as well
  • Additional Notes:

    (Creare's statement)

    Creare seeks Engineering Interns who can support Creare Engineers on a broad spectrum of R&D consulting problems. Excellent record of academic achievement (3.5+ gpa) as well as knowledge of engineering principles. Software programming, analysis, experimental and laboratory skills are required.

    While the thermosciences and associated processes and equipment are core areas, Creare's span of work encompasses the following: Applied Mechanics, Controls, Cryogenics, Data Acquisition, Electronics, Fluid Mechanics, Heat Transfer, Instrumentation, Machine Design, Materials, Medical Devices, Numerical Methods, Signal/Image Processing and Software.

    Creare Inc. is a high technology engineering R&D firm, located in Hanover, New Hampshire, just 2 hours north of Boston, home to Dartmouth College, located in northern New England. With a staff of 120 people, extensive laboratories, machine shops, and experimental facilities and 70+ active projects we offer an exceptional intern experience.
